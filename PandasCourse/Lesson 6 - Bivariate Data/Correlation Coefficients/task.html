Correlation

The pandas DataFrame has an inbuilt method to help quickly find correlations between variables.

Using the .corr() method, pandas lists the correlation coefficients between each column of the DataFrame.
Some examples of correlation coeficients are below

You have been given some data on the number of tickets sold to the school play depending on how many students appeared in the play over a period of 10 years.

Using the .corr() method print out the correlation coefficient between Number of Students and Number of Tickets Sold:
