Grouped Data

You have been provided a survey of 50 people and their journey times via the mode of transport they commute to work.

To group the data by mode of transport the following code can be used:
data.groupby("Mode")

NOTE: this will not return anything unless you add one of the statistical methods, eg .sum(), .count(), .mean(), .max() to the end.
